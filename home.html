Dear friends and family,

Thank you for taking time off your busy schedules to celebrate this special occasion with us. We are incredibly blessed to have such wonderful and loving friends and family.

Here are some details to take note of:

Transport

For those who need transport to and from the venue, there will be coaches leaving from Kowloon Tong and Admiralty MTR station to take you there.

For those who are driving, you need a parking label to park in the Beas River Country Club. We have a limited number of these labels and can offer them on a first-come first-served basis. Please email bettyjustinsghk@gmail.com to have them arranged.

Weather

Please note this is an outdoor, grassy venue and the temperature in March can be chilly (anywhere between 11 and 27 degree celcius).

Dress code

Formal
